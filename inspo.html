Inspiration for the Q-ARCHIVE project

Inspiration

WTF QR CODES

Inspiration for this project includes other digital QR code archives, like WTF QR Codes on Tumblr.

This blog was dedicated to 'stupid' QR codes but still served to keep a log of QR codes that people can scan and go to the cooresponding web page.

This was also a good example of what I want to achieve in the way that it is community based. People could send in photos from wherever they were in the world to be shared on this site.

Dead Drops

Another inspiration for my project is the database for the locations of Dead Drops around the globe. This site features a submission function, as well as a map that users can find their way around.

Because Dead Drops is an offline project, you cannot find any data apart from the location of the USB stick, and photos of its placement on the database.

On Q-ARCHIVE, I want there to be a hyperlink to where the original QR code was found, as well as a clear photo of the original QR code. This way people using the archive can travel on a digital journey through all of the websites that the QR codes lead to.
